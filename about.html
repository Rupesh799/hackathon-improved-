our story

infrastructure vehicle is the future mobile and web app that brings the improvement in the development of infrastructure. this app will have the feature of providing better information about the damaged infrastructure and the work to repair those things. our app will connect with the user who can send the alert message about the damaged infrastructure of the particular location with maps on it. there will be a section of images uploading about that damaged infrastructure. and also have the section of description where we can inform about the things to be repaired. after clicking the alert button, immediately the alert notification will be delivered to the nearby vehicle driver. then he/she will go to that location with the required material and the engineer/technicians/mechanics with the helper or maintenance team.
